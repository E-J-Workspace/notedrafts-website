Main Features

Notes Directly on PDFs

Edit PDFs directly in the app, highlight and comment on important parts. Perfect for students and professionals who need to annotate documents on the go.

Infinite Canvas

Create mind maps, sketches, and diagrams on an infinite canvas. No boundaries for your creativity - zoom, pan, and organize your thoughts freely.

Vertical Notes

Use an endless vertical note area for long lists or continuous writing. Perfect for meeting notes, journaling, or any continuous thought process.

Customizable Templates

Choose from a variety of templates for daily planners, calendars, and habit trackers. Create your own templates to match your unique workflow.
